Aura ensures fairness and transparency through a robust community-driven
voting mechanism used to finalize market outcomes, particularly in cases of
disputes.

## How voting works

When a market outcome is disputed, it enters a community voting phase:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiating a dispute">
    A user initiates a dispute by posting a dispute bond, triggering the
    voting period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Voting section">
    Sign in with your wallet, then access the **Voting** tab from the
    navigation toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a market outcome">
    Review active disputes listed in the **Outcomes** section and cast your
    vote for the outcome you believe is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize results">
    When the voting period ends, the outcome with the most votes becomes
    final. If there is a tie, an immediate revote is triggered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Voting eligibility

- **Neutral voting** — only users who hold no active positions in the
  disputed market can participate, ensuring unbiased outcomes.
- **Voting period** — voting lasts **24 hours** from the moment a dispute is
  initiated.

## Voting power explained

Your voting power is determined by the tier achieved through staking $AURA
tokens:

| Tier | Voting Power |
| --- | --- |
| Degenerate | 30 votes |
| Diamond | 22 votes |
| Platinum | 16 votes |
| Gold | 11 votes |
| Silver | 7 votes |
| Bronze | 3 votes |
| Iron | 1 vote |
| None | 0 votes |

Your voting power directly impacts the resolution of disputed markets,
allowing higher-tiered users greater influence on community-driven outcomes.
