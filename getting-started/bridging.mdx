Your Aura balance is shown in **USD**. Under the hood trading settles in
stablecoins on Alephium, but you never need to think about chains, bridges,
or gas — deposits are credited to your USD balance and withdrawals arrive as
stablecoins wherever you want them.

## Depositing

Open **Deposit** in the top navigation and pick the path that matches where
your money is:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="From another chain" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Send **USDC or USDT** on Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, or BNB Chain to your
    personal deposit address. Most deposits are credited to your USD balance
    within moments of confirming on the source chain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="With a card" icon="credit-card">
    Buy stablecoins with a debit/credit card through the built-in onramp —
    they arrive at your deposit address and are credited the same way.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On Alephium" icon="bolt">
    Already on Alephium? Send USDT directly to your wallet address —
    near-instant, no extra steps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Need help?" icon="life-ring">
    The deposit dialog shows the exact tokens and chains supported right now,
    plus live status for each deposit.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the deposit dialog">
    Click **Deposit** and choose your source chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send to your personal address">
    Copy your deposit address (or scan the QR) and send USDC/USDT from any
    wallet or exchange.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get credited in USD">
    Your balance updates automatically once the deposit confirms — typically
    within moments. Very large deposits can take longer while they are
    routed; the dialog shows their status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Deposit addresses are **yours** — they stay valid, so you can send to the
  same address again next time or withdraw from an exchange straight to it.
</Note>

## Fees

- **Source-chain gas** — you pay the normal network fee on the chain you
  send from (or the card provider's fee for card purchases).
- **Aura deposit fee** — none.

## Withdrawing

Open **Withdraw** and choose where you want the funds:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a destination">
    A supported EVM chain (stablecoins to any address you control) or a
    direct Alephium transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the quote">
    You'll see the exact amount that will arrive, including the network fee,
    before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Withdrawals within the instant window are paid out right away; larger
    amounts are queued and the dialog tracks their status until they land.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What about gas?

You don't hold ALPH (Alephium's gas token) and never see gas prompts.
When an action needs network fees, Aura converts a few cents of your USD
balance automatically — you'll see it as a small "network fees" line, always
denominated in USD. See [Alephium (ALPH)](/tokens/alph) for details.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Deposit not credited yet?">
    - Check the deposit dialog — each deposit shows live status.
    - Confirm the transaction succeeded on the source chain (block explorer).
    - Large deposits above the instant window can take longer while routed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sent an unsupported token or chain?">
    Only send the tokens and chains listed in the deposit dialog. If you sent
    something else, contact support on
    [Telegram](https://t.me/AuraMarkets) with the transaction hash before
    doing anything else.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Withdrawal pending?">
    Instant-window withdrawals settle right away; larger ones show a queued
    status with progress updates. If a withdrawal looks stuck, contact
    support with its ID from the dialog.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
