title: "Connecting to Aura"
description: "One-tap sign-up with a passkey or email — no extensions, no seed phrases."
---

Aura has a built-in wallet, so getting started feels like signing up for any
modern app: no browser extensions, no seed phrases, and no crypto knowledge
required. Your balance is shown in **USD**, and you can fund it from major
chains or a card.

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Aura">
    Visit [aurabets.io](https://aurabets.io/) and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how to sign in">
    Pick **passkey** (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello, or your device PIN) or
    **email** (we send a one-time code).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Your wallet is created instantly with a generated username — you can
    change it any time in **Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

There is nothing to back up. Your passkey (or email) is how you log in from
any device — see [Your Aura Wallet](/getting-started/passkeys) for how the
wallet works under the hood and why it stays fully non-custodial.

<Tip>
  Once connected, head to [Deposits & Withdrawals](/getting-started/bridging)
  to fund your account from Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, BNB Chain, a card, or
  directly on Alephium.
</Tip>
