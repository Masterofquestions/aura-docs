Every Aura account comes with a built-in, **non-custodial** wallet. You never
install anything or write down a seed phrase; you open it with a passkey or
your email, and it works the same on every device.

## How it works

Your keys are generated and held inside **secure hardware enclaves**
(trusted execution environments operated by [Turnkey](https://www.turnkey.com/),
the wallet infrastructure used by major exchanges and apps). The important
part of the design:

- **You are the only authority.** Your wallet lives in its own isolated
  sub-organization where your login methods (passkeys, email) are the sole
  root users. Aura holds no key that can move your funds.
- **Logins, not keys.** A passkey or email code proves it's you and opens a
  short-lived signing session. The private key itself never leaves secure
  hardware — not to your device, not to Aura.
- **No transaction pop-ups.** Once you're signed in, trades and votes sign
  seamlessly in the background. You approve by clicking the action itself,
  the way you'd expect any app to work.

## Signing in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Passkey">
    Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello, or your device PIN. Passkeys sync
    across your devices via iCloud Keychain, Google Password Manager, or your
    Microsoft account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email">
    Enter your email and type in the one-time code we send you. Handy on
    devices that don't share your passkey ecosystem.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Both methods open the **same wallet** — they are ways in, not separate
accounts.

## Your username

Every new account gets a generated username (like `brisk-otter-42`). It's
your public identity on Aura — shown on comments, the leaderboard, and your
profile page. You can rename it, add a profile picture and bio, or make your
profile private in **Settings**.

## Security properties

| Property | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| **No seed phrase** | Nothing to write down, lose, or have stolen |
| **Phishing resistant** | Passkeys are bound to the Aura domain — fake sites can't use them |
| **Hardware-backed keys** | Keys live in secure enclaves, never in your browser |
| **Non-custodial** | Only your login methods can authorize signing — Aura can't move your funds |
| **Cross-device** | Sign in anywhere with your passkey or email |

## Losing access

Because your login methods are independent of any single device:

- **Passkey users** — your passkey syncs through your platform account
  (iCloud, Google, Microsoft). A new phone or laptop just needs that account.
- **Email users** — sign in with a fresh one-time code from any device.

<Warning>
  Your login methods are the only way into your wallet. If you use a single
  passkey ecosystem, consider also completing an email sign-in once so the
  two methods back each other up.
</Warning>
