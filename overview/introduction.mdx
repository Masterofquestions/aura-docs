Aura offers users a seamless, intuitive way to participate in decentralized
prediction markets. Users trade outcome shares predicting future events, with
payouts made in **USDT** for correct predictions.

Our vaulting system, powered by our native token **$AURA**, unlocks multiple
benefits including increased voting power, higher fee-sharing opportunities,
and the ability to propose new prediction markets. Aura is designed for both
newcomers and seasoned traders, providing a clear, rewarding, and
community-driven prediction experience.

## Who Aura is for

- **Traders** — leverage informational edge across crypto, sports, politics,
  and real-world events using a familiar limit order book.
- **Market creators** — propose new markets and earn a tier-scaled share of
  trading fees on every fill.
- **Stakers + voters** — vault $AURA to amplify your influence on dispute
  resolution and proposal approvals, and earn a slice of fees on disputed
  outcomes you call correctly.
- **Builders** — integrate Aura into bots, dashboards, mobile apps, and
  partner products via the [public REST API](/api-reference/introduction).
