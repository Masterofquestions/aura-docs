title: "Why Alephium?"

---

Alephium provides the ideal foundation for Aura due to its advanced blockchain
architecture.

## Efficiency & speed

- Lightning-fast transaction settlement
- Minimal transaction fees, ideal for high-frequency trading

## Scalability

- Sharded BlockFlow architecture supports a growing community without
  sacrificing performance
- Throughput grows with the chain — Aura is not bottlenecked by mainnet
  congestion the way EVM prediction markets are

## User-friendly experience

- Low-cost interactions make Aura accessible to everyone, promoting greater
  participation
- Quick, responsive platform keeps users engaged and informed in real time

Alephium's strengths align perfectly with Aura's mission to offer transparent,
accessible, and rewarding decentralized predictions.
