Your tier significantly impacts your experience and profitability on Aura.
Here's how to make the most of your staked $AURA.

## Stake strategically

- **Aim high** — reach at least the **Gold** tier for notable voting power
  and a substantial share of market fees.
- **Stay informed** — regularly monitor your tier status and adjust your
  staking to maintain the desired benefits.

## Leverage voting power

- **Engage actively** — participate in voting on proposals and disputed
  market outcomes.
- **Strategize** — use your higher-tier influence to shape market quality and
  fairness strategically.

## Earn passive income

- **Market creation** — frequently create markets to capitalize on tier-based
  fee shares (up to 30% for the **Degenerate** tier).
- **Volume matters** — focus on markets that attract significant trading
  volume for steady passive earnings.

## Manage your vault

- **Cooldown awareness** — plan unlock requests carefully, keeping the 7-day
  cooldown period in mind.
- **Strategic withdrawals** — make withdrawals strategically to preserve
  high-tier benefits and maintain platform influence.

## Social sharing tips

- **Spread the word** — share your proposals on social media to gain more
  visibility and votes.
- **Engage your network** — encourage friends and followers to support your
  proposals and discuss them to drive engagement.
- **Visual appeal** — use catchy visuals and concise, compelling captions in
  your shares to attract more attention.

Maximize your $AURA benefits by strategically managing your stake and
actively participating in the Aura community.
