## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Aura?">
    Aura is a decentralized prediction platform built on Alephium, where users
    can predict outcomes of future events, trade shares, and participate in
    community governance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What makes Aura different from other prediction markets?">
    Aura utilizes Alephium's scalable blockchain, offering lower fees, faster
    transactions, and a unique vaulting tier system that provides extensive
    user benefits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Wallet & sign-in

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a wallet?">
    You don't install anything — Aura has a built-in wallet. Click
    **Connect** and sign up with a passkey (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows
    Hello) or your email. Your account is created in one tap with a
    generated username you can change in Settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the wallet non-custodial?">
    Yes. Your keys are generated and held in secure hardware enclaves where
    your login methods (passkeys, email) are the only root authority. Aura
    never holds a key that can move your funds. See
    [Your Aura Wallet](/getting-started/passkeys) for the full design.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see transaction pop-ups?">
    Once you're signed in, actions sign seamlessly in the background —
    clicking **Buy** or **Vote** is the approval. Your login (passkey or
    email code) is what gates access to signing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I lose my device?">
    Passkeys sync across your devices via iCloud (Apple), Google Password
    Manager (Android), or your Microsoft account (Windows) — and email
    sign-in works from any device. As long as you have one login method,
    you have your wallet.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deposits & withdrawals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I deposit from Ethereum or another chain?">
    Click **Deposit**, pick your source chain (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, or
    BNB Chain), and send USDC/USDT to your personal deposit address from any
    wallet or exchange. Your USD balance updates automatically once the
    deposit confirms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long do deposits take?">
    Most deposits are credited within moments of confirming on the source
    chain. Very large deposits can take longer while they're routed — the
    deposit dialog shows live status either way.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there deposit fees?">
    You pay the standard network fee on the chain you send from. Aura does
    not charge any additional deposit fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I buy in with a card?">
    Yes — the deposit dialog includes a card onramp that delivers
    stablecoins straight to your deposit address.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw to Ethereum or another chain?">
    Yes. Click **Withdraw**, pick the destination chain and address, and
    review the quote (which shows the exact amount that will arrive,
    network fee included) before confirming.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Token & vaulting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the $AURA token?">
    $AURA is Aura's governance token. Vaulting (staking) $AURA unlocks voting
    power, fee-sharing opportunities, and market creation privileges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I stake (vault) $AURA tokens?">
    Visit the **Vault** section on the Aura website, input the number of
    tokens you wish to stake, and confirm the transaction via your connected
    wallet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw staked tokens?">
    Yes. You can request to unlock your tokens at any time. Once initiated, a
    mandatory 7-day cooldown applies before withdrawal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Market creation & trading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a new market?">
    Ensure you have at least **Iron**-tier status, navigate to the **Propose
    Market** button, complete the proposal form, and submit it for community
    voting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on how many markets I can propose?">
    Yes — each proposer can have up to **25 proposals outstanding** at a
    time. A slot frees up whenever one of your proposals is finalized
    (approved or rejected). Sibling markets inside a linked bundle don't
    count against the cap — only the bundle's master proposal does.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What fees are involved in trading?">
    A small, transparent **2.5%** trading fee is applied to each transaction
    conducted in USDT.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Market outcomes & disputes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is the outcome of a market determined?">
    Outcomes are initially proposed by users post-market completion and
    finalized through community voting if disputes arise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I disagree with a proposed market outcome?">
    You can initiate a dispute by posting a dispute bond. This action triggers
    a 24-hour community voting period to resolve the outcome.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can vote on disputed outcomes?">
    Anyone with at least **Iron tier** (60,000 vaulted $AURA) at commit
    time can vote. Votes are tier-weighted, and backing a losing outcome
    slashes 0.1% of your vaulted $AURA — the economics, not a position
    check, keep votes honest.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
