## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Aura?">
    Aura is a decentralized prediction platform built on Alephium, where users
    can predict outcomes of future events, trade shares, and participate in
    community governance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What makes Aura different from other prediction markets?">
    Aura utilizes Alephium's scalable blockchain, offering lower fees, faster
    transactions, and a unique vaulting tier system that provides extensive
    user benefits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Passkeys & wallets

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a passkey wallet?">
    A passkey wallet is a non-custodial crypto wallet secured by your device's
    biometrics (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello) instead of a seed phrase.
    It's the easiest way to get started on Aura.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are passkey wallets safe?">
    Yes. Passkeys are fully non-custodial — your private key is stored only
    in your device's secure enclave. Aura never has access to your keys. This
    provides the same security as traditional wallets without the risk of
    losing a seed phrase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my existing Alephium wallet?">
    Absolutely. If you already have an Alephium wallet (browser extension),
    you can connect it directly. Passkeys are simply an additional option for
    easier onboarding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I lose my device?">
    Passkeys sync across your devices via iCloud (Apple), Google Password
    Manager (Android), or Microsoft account (Windows). As long as you have
    access to your cloud account, you can recover your wallet on a new
    device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Bridging & deposits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I deposit USDT from Ethereum or BSC?">
    Use the **Deposit** button in Aura and select your source chain
    (Ethereum or BSC). Connect your EVM wallet (MetaMask, etc.), enter the
    amount, and confirm. The Wormhole bridge will transfer your USDT to Aura
    automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does bridging take?">
    Typically **10–20 minutes**. The time depends on network congestion and
    Wormhole Guardian attestation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there fees for bridging?">
    You pay the standard gas fee on your source chain (ETH or BSC). Aura does
    not charge any additional deposit fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the bridge safe?">
    Yes. Aura uses Wormhole, one of the most trusted bridges in crypto. It's
    secured by 19 independent Guardian validators and has processed billions
    of dollars in transfers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw back to Ethereum or BSC?">
    Yes. Use the **Withdraw** function to bridge your USDT back to Ethereum
    or BSC. The process takes about 10–20 minutes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Token & vaulting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the $AURA token?">
    $AURA is Aura's governance token. Vaulting (staking) $AURA unlocks voting
    power, fee-sharing opportunities, and market creation privileges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I stake (vault) $AURA tokens?">
    Visit the **Vault** section on the Aura website, input the number of
    tokens you wish to stake, and confirm the transaction via your connected
    wallet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw staked tokens?">
    Yes. You can request to unlock your tokens at any time. Once initiated, a
    mandatory 7-day cooldown applies before withdrawal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Market creation & trading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a new market?">
    Ensure you have at least **Iron**-tier status, navigate to the **Propose
    Market** button, complete the proposal form, and submit it for community
    voting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on how many markets I can propose?">
    Yes — tiered users can submit a maximum of 25 market proposals per day,
    with a cooldown period of 1 minute between proposals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What fees are involved in trading?">
    A small, transparent **2.5%** trading fee is applied to each transaction
    conducted in USDT.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Market outcomes & disputes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is the outcome of a market determined?">
    Outcomes are initially proposed by users post-market completion and
    finalized through community voting if disputes arise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I disagree with a proposed market outcome?">
    You can initiate a dispute by posting a dispute bond. This action triggers
    a 24-hour community voting period to resolve the outcome.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can vote on disputed outcomes?">
    Only neutral voters without active positions in the disputed market,
    holding vaulted $AURA tokens, may participate in the outcome voting
    process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
