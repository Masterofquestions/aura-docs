title: "Resolution & Disputes"
description: "How Aura's optimistic oracle settles markets, and how community voting handles disputes."
---

Aura emphasizes transparency and fairness through a structured market
resolution and dispute process, ensuring outcomes reflect community consensus.

## Submitting market outcomes

Once a market reaches its end date, users can submit a proposed outcome
(e.g. **YES** or **NO**) by posting a **100 USDT bond**. This initiates a
24-hour dispute window.

- If no one disputes the proposal within 24 hours, the market automatically
  resolves to that outcome.
- The proposer's 100 USDT bond is refunded in full.

## How disputes work

If another user disagrees with the proposed outcome, they can initiate a
dispute by also staking **100 USDT** within the 24-hour window.

- The market then enters a **24-hour community voting period**.
- During this time, the outcome remains open until the community vote
  concludes.
- Both the proposer and disputer now have 100 USDT at stake — for a total of
  **200 USDT**.

## Voting on outcomes

Only **neutral users** (those with no position in the disputed market) can
vote on the correct outcome. This maintains fairness.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Voting page">
    Visit the **Voting** tab, select the **Outcomes** section, and locate the
    disputed market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cast your vote">
    Voting power is determined by your staking tier (1 to 30 votes, depending
    on tier level — see [Tier Structure](/tokens/tier-structure)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for finalization">
    Majority votes determine the resolution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What if there's a tie?

If the vote results in a tie, the market does **not** resolve. Instead:

- A revote is triggered with another 24-hour voting window.
- This process repeats until a majority is reached.

## Finalizing outcomes

After the voting period concludes:

- **Winning outcome** — determined by majority community votes.
- **Voters are incentivized** — 10% of the market's fees are split among
  users who voted for the final winning outcome, proportional to their voting
  power.
- **Bonds** — the winning side's representative (proposer or disputer)
  receives the full 200 USDT bond as a reward.
- **Outcome enforcement** — winning share holders are automatically paid out
  in USDT.

This transparent, community-driven process ensures integrity and
trustworthiness across all prediction markets on Aura.
