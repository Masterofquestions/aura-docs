Aura provides users the power to propose their own prediction markets.
Community members with a valid staking tier (**Iron** or higher) can create
engaging markets on various events, predictions, and topics.

## Proposal requirements

Before proposing a market, users must meet the following requirements:

- **Minimum tier** — Iron (or higher). Users without a valid tier must vault
  (stake) $AURA tokens first. See [Tier Structure](/tokens/tier-structure) and
  [Staking & Vaulting $AURA](/tokens/staking).
- **Deposit** — a refundable deposit of **0.5 ALPH** per market proposal.

## Market creation process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the proposal form">
    Click the **Propose Market** button at the top-right of the Aura website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the proposal form">
    - **Market question** — clearly state the prediction event or outcome.
    - **Category** — choose the appropriate category to help users find your
      market easily.
    - **Market rules** — provide clear, detailed instructions or conditions
      for resolving the market outcome.
    - **End date** — specify the exact date and time when the market will
      close.
    - **Images and links** — optionally add relevant images or links to
      support clarity and user engagement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your proposal">
    Confirm the submission by authorizing the 0.5 ALPH deposit through your
    wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Community voting">
    Your proposal enters a 24-hour community voting period. Proposals with the
    most votes are considered for approval, but final acceptance also accounts
    for the current number of markets on the platform, recent average daily
    volume, and how many other proposals are active. This means even a
    highly-voted proposal might be rejected if the platform is at capacity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Market limits & restrictions

To maintain platform quality and avoid spam, the following limits apply:

| Limit | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Cooldown between proposals | 1 minute per user |
| Daily proposals per user | 25 |
| Quality assurance | Proposals that don't pass voting and platform criteria are rejected; the proposer's deposit is refunded. |

</Tip>
